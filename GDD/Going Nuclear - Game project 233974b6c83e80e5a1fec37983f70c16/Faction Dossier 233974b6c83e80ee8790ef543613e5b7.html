Faction Dossier

Game Design Document: Faction Dossier

Version 1.1

This document details the playable AI-controlled factions in Project: Going Nuclear

The Superpowers

1. Freedom Inc. (USA)

  • Leader: President Don T. Rump
  • Bio: A real estate mogul turned president, convinced every international treaty is a bad deal that he can renegotiate. Believes "more is more" and that "nuke" is a unit of economic measurement. Tends to see allies as temporary employees.
  • Stats: Pop: 100, Treasury: 200, Morale: 75%, AP: 3
  • Personality: UNPREDICTABLE, AGGRESSIVE, CHARISMATIC
  • Unique Ability: Economic Sanctions. (Action) Spend 1 AP and 50 Treasury to apply a "Sanctioned" status effect to a target, halving their Treasury gain for 3 turns.

2. The Mother-lode (Russia)

  • Leader: President-for-Life Vlad 'The Impaler' Puttin
  • Bio: A former intelligence agent with a penchant for shirtless horseback riding and rewriting history. Views international borders as suggestions and believes the only thing better than a strong hand is a stronger poison.
  • Stats: Pop: 120, Treasury: 100, Morale: 85%, AP: 3
  • Personality: RUTHLESS, PATIENT, DEFENSIVE
  • Unique Ability: Siberian Resolve. (Passive) Takes 50% less population damage from InfoWar attacks and is immune to the "Fallout" status effect from Dirty Bombs.

3. The People's Social Harmony Co. (China)

  • Leader: Eternal Chairman Xi
  • Bio: A leader with a 100-year plan for everything. Believes in peace and harmony, which is best achieved when all neighboring countries are heavily in debt to him. His approval rating is always 101%.
  • Stats: Pop: 150, Treasury: 175, Morale: 80%, AP: 4
  • Personality: STRATEGIC, EXPANSIONIST, PATIENT
  • Unique Ability: Belt and Loan Initiative. (Action) Spend 2 AP to give a target 50 Treasury. Their relationship with you permanently increases, but their relationship with the USA permanently decreases.

The European Powers

4. The Once-and-Future Empire (UK)

  • Leader: Prime Minister 'Bojo' Jumbling
  • Bio: A man whose haircut is his primary foreign policy. Believes any problem can be solved with a bit of bluster, a witty quote in Latin, and a conveniently ignored detail. Fond of big red buses and even bigger promises.
  • Stats: Pop: 80, Treasury: 120, Morale: 85%, AP: 3
  • Personality: OPPORTUNIST, CHARISMATIC, UNPREDICTABLE
  • Unique Ability: Muddle Through. (Passive) The first time this faction's population drops below 40, it is immediately restored to 60 at the start of their next turn.

5. La République Chic (France)

  • Leader: Président Emmanuel 'Le Grand' Macaron
  • Bio: A philosopher-king who believes a well-tailored suit is the first line of defense. A firm believer in strategic autonomy, especially if it means holding a separate, more exclusive summit right after the main one.
  • Stats: Pop: 85, Treasury: 140, Morale: 90%, AP: 4
  • Personality: DIPLOMATIC, PROUD
  • Unique Ability: Vive La Résistance! (Passive) When this faction is the target of a conventional attack, the attacker suffers 5M population loss as "retaliation," regardless of the attack's success.

6. The Euro-Zone Industrial Complex (Germany)

  • Leader: Chancellor Klaus von Efficiency
  • Bio: A pragmatist who views the world as a complex machine that is simply not running on time. Believes every problem has a technical manual and a sensible, risk-averse, multi-year solution. Abhors waste, spontaneity, and unbalanced budgets.
  • Stats: Pop: 90, Treasury: 220, Morale: 80%, AP: 2
  • Personality: DEFENSIVE, PATIENT
  • Unique Ability: Wirtschaftswunder. (Passive) Gains +20% bonus Treasury each turn, but their conventional attack cards cost +1 AP to play.

7. The Gesticulating Republic (Italy)

  • Leader: Prime Minister Silvio 'Bunga Bunga' Bellissimo
  • Bio: A flamboyant media tycoon who insists he is only in politics as a public service and for the parties. His foreign policy changes depending on who he spoke to last and is always expressed with passionate hand gestures.
  • Stats: Pop: 75, Treasury: 110, Morale: 85%, AP: 3
  • Personality: CHARISMATIC, UNPREDICTABLE, OPPORTUNIST
  • Unique Ability: Political Chaos. (Passive) InfoWar attacks targeting this faction have a 50% chance to fail and be completely ignored as "just another headline."

The Wildcards & Regional Powers

8. The Democratic People's Rocket Club (North Korea)

  • Leader: Supreme Leader Kim Jong Fun
  • Bio: A third-generation dictator with a love for cheese, military parades, and threatening letters. His primary goal is to get the attention of the major powers, usually by test-firing a new "super-duper-mega-missile" into the sea.
  • Stats: Pop: 40, Treasury: 20, Morale: 90%, AP: 2
  • Personality: ISOLATIONIST, AGGRESSIVE, UNPREDICTABLE
  • Unique Ability: Juche Superweapon. (Action) Can spend their entire turn (all AP) to launch a special attack. It has a 50% chance to be a massive success (double damage) and a 50% chance to be a total dud (the missile explodes on the launchpad, damaging their own population).

9. The Divine Mandate (Iran)

  • Leader: Supreme Theocrat Ali Khameanie
  • Bio: An austere and powerful cleric who plays the long game. He is a master of asymmetric warfare, believing it's much cheaper and more effective to have someone else fight your battles for you.
  • Stats: Pop: 70, Treasury: 60, Morale: 80%, AP: 3
  • Personality: RUTHLESS, DEFENSIVE, PATIENT
  • Unique Ability: Proxy War. (Action) Spend 1 AP and 30 Treasury to target two other AI players. They both lose 10M population and their relationship with each other decreases.

10. Bharat Unlimited (India)

  • Leader: Prime Minister Narinder 'The Tiger' Moody
  • Bio: A charismatic and powerful nationalist leader who is overseeing a billion-person economic and technological boom. He is determined to have his nation take its place as a global superpower.
  • Stats: Pop: 160, Treasury: 120, Morale: 70%, AP: 3
  • Personality: EXPANSIONIST, PROUD, AGGRESSIVE
  • Unique Ability: Demographic Dividend. (Passive) At the start of every turn, this faction gains +2M population automatically.

11. The State of Pak-istan (Pakistan)

  • Leader: Generalissimo Al-Pocalypse
  • Bio: A perpetually concerned general who heads a nation with a powerful military and a complex relationship with all of its neighbors. He is a master of playing superpowers against each other to his own advantage.
  • Stats: Pop: 60, Treasury: 70, Morale: 75%, AP: 3
  • Personality: OPPORTUNIST, TREACHEROUS
  • Unique Ability: Nuclear Black Market. (Action) Can give one of their PAYLOAD cards to another AI player. If the offer is accepted, this faction gains a large amount of Treasury, and their relationship with the receiving faction improves.

12. The Iron Dome Corporation (Israel)

  • Leader: Prime Minister 'Bibi' Not-on-your-yahoo
  • Bio: A shrewd and tough political survivor who views national security as a 24/7 high-tech startup. His nation is small but punches far above its weight, with world-class intelligence and defensive systems.
  • Stats: Pop: 30, Treasury: 150, Morale: 90%, AP: 4
  • Personality: DEFENSIVE, STRATEGIC, RUTHLESS
  • Unique Ability: Mossad Operation. (Action) Spend 1 AP and 40 Treasury to look at a target player's hand and force them to discard one card of your choice.

13. The Neo-Ottoman Confederacy (Turkey)

  • Leader: Sultan Recep 'The Magnificent' Tayyipalooza
  • Bio: A leader who dreams of past glories and a future where he is at the center of everything. He is a master of balancing relationships with all sides, often threatening one ally to get concessions from another.
  • Stats: Pop: 80, Treasury: 90, Morale: 80%, AP: 3
  • Personality: AGGRESSIVE, DIPLOMATIC, OPPORTUNIST
  • Unique Ability: Crossroads of the World. (Passive) This faction can never have a relationship score with any other faction drop below -5. They are too strategically important to be completely alienated.

14. The Unbreakable Nation (Ukraine)

  • Leader: President Volodomyr 'The Unyielding' Zelenko
  • Bio: A former entertainer who became a globally recognized symbol of defiance and courage. His nation's greatest weapon is its unbreakable will, but it is heavily reliant on outside support to keep the war machine running.
  • Stats: Pop: 60, Treasury: 40, Morale: 95%, AP: 4
  • Personality: DEFENSIVE, CHARISMATIC, RUTHLESS
  • Unique Ability: Foreign Aid. (Action) Spend 2 AP to make a plea to the "International Community" (the game). A random AI Faction with a positive relationship with you will be forced to give you a portion of their Treasury.

15. The Anime & Robotics Shogunate (Japan)

  • Leader: Prime Minister Fumio 'Salaryman' Kishida
  • Bio: The soft-spoken leader of a technological and economic giant. While publicly committed to pacifism, his nation's "Self-Defense Force" possesses some of the most advanced defensive and robotic technology on the planet, all developed under the guise of "disaster relief."
  • Stats: Pop: 95, Treasury: 180, Morale: 80%, AP: 2
  • Personality: DEFENSIVE, STRATEGIC
  • Unique Ability: Advanced Robotics. (Passive) All DEFENSE type cards played by this faction are 50% more effective or have a chance to not be discarded after use.
